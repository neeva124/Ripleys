About Ripley's Aquarium

Ripley's Aquarium of Canada is a public aquarium in Toronto, Ontario. It is owned by Ripley Entertainment and located near the CN Tower, offering an unparalleled experience of aquatic wonders. Ripley's Aquarium offers a mesmerizing underwater adventure that delights visitors of all ages. Located in the heart of Toronto, the aquarium features over 20,000 marine animals, from graceful sharks and stingrays to vibrant tropical fish. Highlights include the thrilling walk through the Dangerous Lagoon, where you can watch sea creatures glide overhead, and interactive exhibits that allow guests to touch stingrays and horseshoe crabs. With its captivating displays, educational programs, and breathtaking aquatic environments, Ripley's Aquarium provides an unforgettable journey into the wonders of the ocean.

Major Attractions

Jellyfish

Ethereal jellyfish drift gracefully, showcasing their translucent bodies and mesmerizing bioluminescence, captivating visitors with their serene beauty.

Starfish

Vibrant starfish, with unique shapes and colors, demonstrate the importance of marine biodiversity while intriguing guests with their fascinating adaptations.

Stingrays

At Ripley's Aquarium, visitors can observe graceful stingrays up close and even interact with them in touch tanks, offering a unique, hands-on experience.

Seahorse

Seahorses are captivating marine creatures known for their distinctive horse-like heads, ability to change color, and unique reproductive role, where males carry and nurture their young.

Giant Octopus

The giant octopus captivates with its intelligence, color-changing abilities, and playful nature, offering a glimpse into underwater mystery.
